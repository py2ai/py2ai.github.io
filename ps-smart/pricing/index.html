Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay for AI API usage?
PS Smart uses your own API keys for cloud providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.). You pay those providers directly. For local AI with Ollama, there are no API costs.
Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and billing is prorated.
Is there a free trial for Pro?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial of Pro features. No credit card required. Contact us to start your trial.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
