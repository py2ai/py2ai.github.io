Energy Calculation Method

Power and Energy are calculated using:

  • Power = Rated Wattage × (Irradiance/1000) × System Efficiency
  • STC = Standard Test Conditions (1000 W/m²)
  • Sun position and atmospheric transmittance
  • Panel tilt and azimuth angles
  • System Efficiency accounts for inverter, wiring losses

Note: This is an estimate. Actual output varies by weather, shading, panel age, and maintenance.

Panel Azimuth Definition

Panel Azimuth is the horizontal angle between true north and the direction the solar panel faces.

  • 0° = Panel faces North
  • 90° = Panel faces East
  • 180° = Panel faces South
  • 270° = Panel faces West

Optimal: South (180°) for Northern Hemisphere, North (0°) for Southern Hemisphere.
