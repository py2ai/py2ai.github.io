"When it comes to solar energy, there's a simple truth: 'Make hay while the sun shines.'

To capture the most sunlight, solar panels must be positioned using key angles.

First is the tilt angle - the angle between the panel and the ground. Our calculator sets this equal to your latitude for optimal year-round performance.

Next is the sun altitude angle, showing how high the sun rises above the horizon.

The zenith angle measures between the sun and the vertical direction above your panel.

Finally, the azimuth angle tells us the sun's direction along the horizon - South for the Northern Hemisphere, North for the Southern."
